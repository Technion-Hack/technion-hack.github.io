About us

Arrhythmia is an abnormal cardiac rhythm that affects the pattern and rate of the heartbeat. To date, electrocardiogram (ECG) analysis is still considered the gold standard for the diagnosis of arrhythmias. While the ECG signal is very informative, its use is mainly restricted inside clinics. With the rising popularity of wearable devices with the ability of measuring and storing heart rate data comes an opportunity to increase early detection and monitoring of arrhythmias. Since these devices allow continuous monitoring, and their prevalence in the general population is increasingly growing, arrythmia detection solutions based on wearable technology have the potential to significantly increase the early detection of arrhythmias and facilitate their monitoring.

Our Goal

To develop a tool for the detection of arrhythmias that relies solely on heart rate data extracted from wearable devices, allowing users to visualize, analyze and identify indications of arrhythmias in their data.

How can you help?

To achieve our goal, we need to collect real heart rate data extracted from wearable devices which we will be using for testing and evaluation. We are looking for people who are willing to contribute to our project by sharing their data with us.
